Interesting Links to CIA Information and Research

Here is a list of links (URLs) to information, videos, documents, and web sites that I find interesting.

Nakhon Phanom (NKP aka Naked Fanny) was a very secret base in Thailand where much of the bombing and surveillance aircraft to flatten the HCMT were based here.
