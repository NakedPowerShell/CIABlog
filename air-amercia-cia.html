Air Amercia : CIA

CIA Taxi and Cargo Service

Air America (AA) was a large private airline that was owned by the CIA. AA flew a large variety of special aircraft into very dangerous situations, carrying food like bags of rice, people ( both civilians and "customers i.e. CIA" ), medical supplies, ammo, fuel, and animals like oxen, pigs, and chickens.

Air America: CIA

AA was like a taxi and cargo service on demand for any CIA people or operation.

Some of the Aircraft they flew were STOL (Short Takeoff and Landing) aircraft. They are extremely handy at landing on tiny runways in the jungle and taking back off.
