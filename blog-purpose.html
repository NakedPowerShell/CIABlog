An Insider’s Look at the CIA.

This online blog about the CIA is like no other. It details the bizarre behavior of my parents during and after the time that they both worked for the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA).

Written from the perspective of a young man inside this family, watching the world around me. Imagine your Father being a top secret spy and your Mom is an administrative CIA employee that handles super classified documents. As a family, we traveled extensively and met a wide range of Spies, Diplomats, and Mercenaries.
