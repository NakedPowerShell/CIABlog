Why Laos Stories Mostly

Why Laos All the Time?

Why so much about Laos because you did live other places too?

* Only war zone as a CIA family member I have been to
* Only CIA conflict I got to see up close and personal
* I was 11-13 so my memories are deeper
* I will talk about Germany, Italy, Virginia, Pennsylvania, North Carolina

Laos effected my Family and myself profoundly, with lingering issues even today.

I have a type of PTSD because I was just a kid and not prepared to witness all the death, destruction, fear, and anxiousness. My Father did not handle the pressures well and he started to unravel.

While the family was in the CIA, we first moved to:

* Fairfax Virginia
* Frankfurt Germany
* Fairfax Virginia
* Vientiane Laos
* Rome Italy
* Fairfax Virginia
* Texas: When my Father retired

I was asked recently why the CIA would send an entire family into a war zone like that.

I did not have an answer.
